The sample text, timing data, generated audio, and generated cover image in this repository are original demo assets dedicated to the public domain under `CC0-1.0`.

The audio in `media/sample.m4a` was generated from the text in `data/small-walk.json` with FFmpeg's `flite` filter. The cover in `media/cover.png` was generated with FFmpeg drawing filters.

No third-party book text, audiobook recording, or cover art is included in the sample.
